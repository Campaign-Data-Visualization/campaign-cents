KEY RACES

The Republicans need to gain only six seats to take the Senate and therefore have control over both houses of congress. The Koch brothers stand to gain more from this than anybody, as they would be perfectly positioned to use their influence over the Tea Party Caucus to push through legislation to deregulate the energy industry and privatize social services. These races will prove most critical to deciding the fate of the Senate.

ORGs TO WATCH

One of the most destructive ways the Koch brothers spread their ideals and infiltrate Washington’s political scene is through the use of Koch-funded think tanks, Astroturf agents (fake grassroots groups), and lobbyists. IFG has compiled a list of “Orgs to Watch,” which are organizations and groups that are backed by Koch cash and have a heavy influence in American politics to spread awareness of whose voices are actually being heard in our democracy.
